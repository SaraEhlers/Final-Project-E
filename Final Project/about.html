Storybook Company

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe exersions into the your favorite fairtale lands, and to educate the general public abou the various mythical creatures that can be encountered on your adventures.

Storybook History

Established in 1966 by our CEO Benin "King" Arthur with the assistance of a team of "knights of the Round Table", the company set out to give the commonfolk the experiences of the fairytales from their childhoods. It started with one campground, the Mystic Lake, and expanded to the three we currently have today.
